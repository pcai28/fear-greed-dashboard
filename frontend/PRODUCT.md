# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Casual investors and active traders who need a fast, comprehensible view of US stock market sentiment. They use the dashboard to understand the current market mood, place it in historical context, and support investment research without wading through financial-news noise.

## Product Purpose

Bring CNN Fear & Greed and VIX data together in one clear, scannable dashboard so users can understand today's sentiment, recognize historical extremes, and make better-informed investment decisions. Success means users can extract the important signal in a single visit and trust what the interface communicates. A future sentiment-alert service may notify subscribed users when meaningful extremes occur, subject to demonstrated user interest.

## Brand Personality

Trustworthy, approachable, clear, and calm. The product should pair the information density and scanability of TradingView with the restraint and legibility associated with Apple, while speaking plainly to investors with different levels of experience.

## Anti-references

Never resemble a clickbait financial-news website, an ad-heavy content site, or an overwhelming trading terminal. Avoid sensational claims, attention traps, decorative market noise, and components that do not improve understanding or decision-making.

## Design Principles

- Make the market's mood legible at a glance, then reveal context for deeper research.
- Earn trust through clear sourcing, honest data states, and restrained language.
- Optimize every component for understanding or decision-making; remove anything that serves neither.
- Support both quick scanning and deliberate comparison without overwhelming the user.
- Preserve the established blue-and-orange identity while keeping meaning independent of color alone.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA from the beginning. Build for keyboard navigation, screen readers, reduced-motion preferences, and color-vision differences. Charts and status indicators must use redundant cues beyond color, and all essential market information must remain perceivable and understandable across supported devices and input methods.
