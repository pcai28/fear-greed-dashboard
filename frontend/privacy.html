US Stock Market Emotions

Privacy Policy

Effective July 3, 2026

US Stock Market Emotions is a market-sentiment dashboard operated by the person or company identified before waitlist launch (“we,” “us,” or “our”). This policy explains the limited personal information we handle. The dashboard and its launch waitlist are available globally.

Information we handle

  • Your email address and the date and version of your waitlist consent.
  • A short-lived, pseudonymous rate-limit identifier derived from your IP address.
  • Security-check data processed by Cloudflare Turnstile when you use the waitlist form.
  • Your theme preference, stored only in your browser's local storage.

We currently do not use advertising or analytics trackers, sell or share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising, or build cross-site advertising profiles. We do not collect passwords or payment information.

How and why we use information

We use a waitlist email only to send one notification when Market Sentiment SMS Alerts launch. The legal basis for this email use is your consent, which you may withdraw at any time. We use short-lived rate-limit identifiers for our legitimate interest in protecting the API from abuse. Cloudflare Turnstile helps us distinguish legitimate waitlist signups from automated abuse. Theme preference is stored locally and used only to display the site in your chosen appearance.

Service providers and disclosures

MongoDB Atlas stores waitlist records. Upstash processes short-lived rate-limit identifiers and cached public market data. Our hosting provider serves the site and may process standard access logs. Cloudflare processes the security check used by the waitlist form. Our business email provider processes privacy requests and the one launch email. Yahoo and CNN market-data requests do not receive your email or waitlist record. We may also disclose information if required by law or necessary to protect the service.

Retention

When the launch notice is sent, or if the project is cancelled, waitlist records are scheduled for automatic deletion within 30 days. We review unnotified waitlist records and our service providers at least quarterly.

Your choices and requests

Depending on applicable law, you may withdraw consent or ask to access, correct, delete, restrict, object to processing of, or receive a portable copy of your waitlist record. We verify a request by confirming control of the email address involved and respond within the time required by applicable law. Contact us at the business contact email listed before waitlist launch. You may also lodge a complaint with the data-protection authority where you live or work. We do not discriminate against anyone for making a privacy request.

Tracking signals and third-party tracking

We currently do not track visitors across third-party websites or permit third parties to do so through this site. Because the current site does not sell or share personal information or perform cross-site tracking, browser Do Not Track and Global Privacy Control signals do not change its behavior.

We may introduce advertising, analytics, or other data-supported features in the future. Before doing so, we will update this policy to describe the information involved, the providers, purposes, retention periods, and choices available. Where applicable law requires it, we will ask for consent before activating non-essential tracking, provide a way to opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information, and honor recognized opt-out preference signals such as Global Privacy Control. We will not use information already collected under this policy for a materially different purpose without any notice or consent required by law. Waitlist consent covers only the one launch notification and does not authorize advertising, data sales, or cross-site profiling.

Children

The waitlist is not directed to children under 16. If we learn that a child's information was submitted without any consent required by applicable law, we will delete it.

Security and international transfers

We use access controls, encrypted service connections, secret management, input limits, and short retention periods. No internet service can guarantee absolute security. Our hosting, MongoDB, Upstash, and business email providers may process information in the United States. Where required, international transfers rely on the providers' applicable data-processing terms and contractual safeguards. Contact us to request information about those safeguards. We do not use automated decision-making or profiling with legal or similarly significant effects.

Policy changes and contact

Material changes will be posted here with a new effective date before they apply to newly collected information. If you have a question about this policy or want to exercise a privacy right, email us at the business contact email listed before waitlist launch.
