How these indicators relate

Fear & Greed summarizes current risk appetite, while VIX reflects expected volatility over the next 30 days. Read them together for context—not as standalone trading signals.

CNN Fear & Greed Index definition

The CNN Fear & Greed Index is a composite market sentiment indicator that combines multiple stock market, bond, options, and safe-haven demand signals into a 0-100 score. Lower values indicate stronger fear, while higher values indicate stronger greed.

VIX (CBOE Volatility Index) definition

The VIX is a 30-day expected volatility index calculated by CBOE from S&P 500 option prices. It is often called the market's fear gauge. Higher values generally indicate greater expected volatility and stronger investor uncertainty.
