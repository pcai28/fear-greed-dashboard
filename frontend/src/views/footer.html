About This Dashboard

The page refreshes data automatically about every 10 minutes while it is open. VIX can update during regular market hours, while the Fear & Greed Index may update less frequently and may reflect provider-side delays. Dates and times are displayed in the viewer's local browser timezone.

Methodology

Fear & Greed and VIX are useful together because they look at market emotion from different angles. Fear & Greed summarizes broad risk appetite on a 0-100 scale, while VIX reflects expected S&P 500 volatility from options pricing. When Fear & Greed falls and VIX rises, both often point to rising uncertainty; when Fear & Greed rises and VIX falls, markets are often calmer and more risk-seeking.
